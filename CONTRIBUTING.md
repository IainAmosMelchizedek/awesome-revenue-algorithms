# Contributing to Awesome Revenue Algorithms

Thanks for wanting to contribute!

### What we want
- Algorithms/libraries that have demonstrated revenue impact
- Clear, concise entries in the format: `**[Tool/Library Name](link)** — one-line description of monetization value.`
- Real-world case studies or benchmarks preferred

### How to add something
1. Fork & branch
2. Add your entry to the correct section in README.md
3. Submit a PR

We keep this list focused and high-signal.
